Common Questions

Quick answers for the front desk

"Does it hurt?" Most treatments feel like tiny pinches. We use topical numbing for comfort.

"When will I see results?" Botox: 3-7 days. Fillers: immediate. Facials: right away. Peels: after peeling stops.

"Can I work out after?" No heavy exercise same day for injectables. Facials and peels are fine.

"What's the cancellation policy?" 24 hours notice to avoid charges.
