Pre-Treatment Prep

What clients should know

24 Hours Before Injectables
Avoid alcohol, aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil, vitamin E
Day of Facials
Come with clean skin, no retinoids for 3 days prior
Laser Appointments
No sun exposure, no self-tanner for 2 weeks
CoolSculpting
Eat normally, wear comfortable clothes

Always review medical history and medications. When in doubt, have them consult with the provider first.
